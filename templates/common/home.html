About us

FarmHouse is your go to app to get fresh canadian produce. This app allows the Farmers in Ontario to sell their products online allowing you to get fresh and organic produce directly from Canadian farms. The system has been designed to purchase all kinds of fresh produce directly from the canadian farmers eleminating the middleman. At present users may face difficulty in purchasing genuine organic products.
